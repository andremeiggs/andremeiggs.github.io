Rock

Rock is one of my favorite genres. My favorite artist is Dave Grohl,
and my favorite band is Foo Fighters. Their music energizes me and deeply resonates with my emotions.
Below is a list of Foo Fighters' albums:

  • Foo Fighters (1995)
  • The Colour and the Shape (1997)
  • There Is Nothing Left to Lose (1999)
  • One by One (2002)
  • In Your Honor (2005)
  • Echoes, Silence, Patience & Grace (2007)
  • Wasting Light (2011)
  • Sonic Highways (2014)
  • Concrete and Gold (2017)
  • Medicine at Midnight (2021)

Salsa

Salsa is a genre I love for its contagious rhythm and the energy it brings.
Artists like Héctor Lavoe and Marc Anthony are some of my favorites.

  • La Fania All-Stars
  • Gilberto Santa Rosa
  • Rubén Blades

Merengue

Merengue always puts me in a good mood. I enjoy artists like Juan Luis Guerra,
who combines beautiful lyrics with vibrant melodies.

It is perfect for dancing and having fun with friends.

Ballad

Ballads connect me with my emotions. Songs by artists like Luis Miguel and Ricardo Montaner always manage to convey deep feelings.

It is a perfect genre for moments of relaxation or introspection.
